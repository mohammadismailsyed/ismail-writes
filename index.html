About Organic Farming

Building a Sustainable Future

Organic farming is an agricultural method that avoids synthetic fertilizers and pesticides while promoting soil health, biodiversity, and environmental balance.

This Smart Organic Farming Management System helps farmers and students understand crop selection, cost estimation, and sustainable farming practices through a modern web platform.

The project demonstrates the application of web technologies in agriculture and serves as a foundation for future AI-powered farming systems.
